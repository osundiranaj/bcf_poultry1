🚀 Registration Steps

1

Create Account

Visit booking.html, click "Register Farm", and fill in your basic information (name, phone, email, location).

2

Verify Identity

Upload your ID document and proof of address. BCF team will verify within 24-48 hours.

3

Complete Farm Profile

Add farm details: capacity, bird types, current stock, facilities, and photos of your farm.

4

Set Preferences

Configure your feed requirements, preferred investors, booking rules, and payment settings.

5

Get Approved

After BCF review, your farm goes live on the directory. You'll receive a welcome email with next steps.

6

Start Using Platform

List available birds, request feed supplies, accept investment offers, and manage your poultry business.

📖 Platform Glossary

Bird Maturity Index

A calculated metric showing the growth progress of birds toward optimal processing weight.

Feed Conversion Ratio (FCR)

The amount of feed required to produce one kilogram of bird weight gain. Lower FCR = better efficiency.

Escrow Payment

Secure payment holding system where funds are released only after agreed conditions are met.

BCF Verification Badge

A trust indicator showing that a user, farm, or business has passed BCF's quality and compliance checks.

Investment Matching

The platform's algorithm that pairs investors with farmers based on risk profile, location, and investment goals.
