Conference Focus Areas

Comprehensive coverage of critical topics shaping the future of poultry supply chain in Africa

Feed Support & Financing

Exploring innovative financing models, feed subsidy programs, investment opportunities, and sustainable feed supply chain solutions for small to large-scale poultry farmers.

  • Feed credit facilities
  • Investor-farmer matching
  • Cost optimization strategies

Logistics & Resource Import

Streamlining poultry logistics, cold chain management, import procedures for equipment and feed ingredients, and distribution network optimization across West Africa.

  • Cold chain solutions
  • Import documentation
  • Last-mile delivery

Quality Scaling & Standards

Implementing international quality standards, certification processes, biosecurity measures, and scaling operations while maintaining product quality and food safety.

  • Quality certification
  • Biosecurity protocols
  • Best practices

Banking & Investment

Connecting farmers with financial institutions, exploring venture capital opportunities, understanding agricultural loans, and building bankable poultry business models.

  • Agricultural loans
  • VC funding
  • ROI optimization

Regional Trade & Export

Navigating AfCFTA opportunities, cross-border trade regulations, export procedures, and building pan-African poultry supply chains for regional and international markets.

  • AfCFTA opportunities
  • Export documentation
  • Market access

Technology & Innovation

  • Farm management tech

  • Digital payments

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the conference

What is included in the conference fee?
The conference fee includes access to all keynote sessions, workshop sessions (for Premium pass holders), conference materials, networking sessions, exhibition hall access, refreshments, lunch for all 3 days, and certificate of attendance. Premium pass holders also get VIP networking dinner, conference recordings, and year-round platform access.
Can I get a refund if I can't attend?
Yes, we offer a flexible cancellation policy. Full refund if cancelled 60 days before the event, 50% refund if cancelled 30-60 days before, and no refund for cancellations less than 30 days before the event. However, you can transfer your ticket to another person at no cost.
Will there be accommodation arrangements?
We have partnered with several hotels in Ibadan to offer special discounted rates for conference attendees. Upon registration, you'll receive a list of partner hotels with booking codes. We also provide shuttle service from major hotels to the conference venue.
Is there a virtual attendance option?
Yes! We offer a virtual attendance package for ₦25,000 which includes live streaming of all keynote sessions, access to digital materials, virtual networking rooms, and conference recordings. However, in-person attendance is highly recommended for the full networking and workshop experience.
How can I become a speaker or exhibitor?
If you're interested in speaking or exhibiting, please contact us at speakers@bcfpoultry.com or exhibitors@bcfpoultry.com respectively. We review all proposals and will get back to you within 2 weeks. Exhibition spaces are limited and allocated on a first-come, first-served basis.
Will I receive a certificate?
Yes, all attendees will receive a Certificate of Attendance at the end of the conference. This certificate can be used for professional development credits and continuing education requirements.
